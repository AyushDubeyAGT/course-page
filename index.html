Things people often ask about

  • How much does Wiza cost?

    We offer a pay as you go plan, it’s 15 cents per valid email! We also have a pro plan, for $50/month you get Integrations, 300 leads per month, and additional leads at 10 cents per valid email.

  • Do I need a Sales Navigator account?

    Having a sales navigator helps a lot with uncovering people outside your network and it makes it easier for Wiza to get you solid results. If you don't have an account, Linkedin offers a free trial.

  • How do I get charged exactly?

    You only get charged for each valid email. Wiza estimates the cost before you run a search and the final charge is often less. We also provide risky emails for free!

  • Can I use Wiza with any browser?

    Wiza requires you to download our chrome extension, available here. That means, you must be on Google Chrome to use Wiza.

  • How do I get my leads?

    After finishing a scrape with Wiza, your leads will be available for download as a csv file in your account dashboard, you can also export your leads directly to Salesforce.

  • Can I export saved lead lists?

    Yes! You can export custom searches, and saved lead lists. As you normally would, just click "Export Leads with Wiza" in your saved list, and we'll do the rest!
